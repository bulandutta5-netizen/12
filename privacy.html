Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Curious Minds Academy is committed to respecting and protecting the privacy rights of our students, parents, and website users. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, store, share, and utilize your personal information when you browse our website or enroll in our academic courses.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you submit directly to our inquiry forms or admission wizards, including:

  • Student's full name, date of birth, gender, and residential address.
  • Parent or guardian's name, mobile phone number, and email address.
  • Current academic grade, board, and course preferences.
  • Feedback, reviews, and newsletter subscriptions.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized strictly for educational and administrative purposes:

  • Processing your online admissions and course enrollments.
  • Contacting you regarding demo sessions, counseling, and fee charts.
  • Sending weekly academic mock test progress alerts to parents.
  • Improving our website usability, newsletter tips, and services.

4. Information Sharing & Security

We do NOT sell, lease, or rent your personal information to third-party marketing agencies. All records are stored securely in encrypted databases. Access is limited strictly to authorized academic and administration staff of Curious Minds Academy.

5. Cookies Policy

Our website utilizes cookies to maintain your theme preferences (Dark/Light mode toggle state) and analyze general web traffic. You can choose to disable cookies in your web browser settings if desired.
