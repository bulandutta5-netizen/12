Infrastructure

Grooming Students in Modern Ambience

Curious Minds Academy features a curated infrastructure optimized to facilitate concentration, interactive learning, and zero distractions.

AC Classrooms

Comfortable, silent, and fully air-conditioned rooms designed to foster focus and prevent hot-weather fatigue.

Digital Smart Boards

Visual aids, graphical models, and conceptual slides displayed on interactive smart screens to boost engagement.

Reference Library

Silent study desk zones featuring standard text directories, competition papers, and JEE/NEET manuals.

Printed Notes & Books

Well-researched, chapter-wise summary notes, conceptual sheets, and formulas directories provided to every student.

Regular Mock Test Series

Comprehensive descriptive board drills and computer-like MCQs mimicking exact national entrance exam blueprints.

Computer Lab

Equipped workstations for national JEE/NEET simulated online test practicing and report submissions.

Doubt Solving Desk

Dedicated personal cabins where students can walk up to core subject experts and solve specific sheet questions.

Personal Lockers

Secure cubby cabinets where students can leave heavy manuals, test registries, or drawing instruments safely.
