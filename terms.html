1. Batch Enrollment and Fees

Admissions into regular and competitive coaching batches are subject to vacancy availability and counseling evaluations. All fees must be paid in full or in agreed installments before the course commencement. Fees once paid are non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances.

2. Student Code of Conduct

Students are expected to maintain strict discipline, decorum, and focus inside the campus. Continuous absence for more than 3 consecutive lectures without parent prior intimation will trigger review actions. CMA reserves the right to terminate registration of any student showing inappropriate or destructive behavior.

3. Course Materials Usage

All study materials, printed summary notes, mock papers, and digital question sheets provided by CMA are copyrighted. Sharing, scanning, uploading, or distributing these resources to external parties or digital channels is strictly prohibited and subject to legal actions.

4. Mock Tests Attendance

Weekly Sunday mock evaluations are mandatory for all students enrolled in CBSE boards, JEE, and NEET coaching programs. Continuous skip of mock tests without valid medical or family grounds will trigger warnings and mandatory parent conferences.

5. Limitation of Liability

While Curious Minds Academy provides high-yield coaching materials, experienced mentors, and evaluation trackers, final academic success relies heavily on the student's individual practice and hard work. CMA does not guarantee fixed ranks or admissions in specific government/IIT colleges.
