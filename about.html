Director's Desk Message

Nurturing Minds, Building Careers

Welcome to Curious Minds Academy. For over 11 years, we have worked with a singular focus: to ignite a curiosity for learning in every student. We believe that education is not merely about memorizing facts; it is about building deep, conceptual foundations that empower students to analyze, reason, and lead.

Our curriculum is structured to bridge school education with competitive expectations, enabling students to excel in CBSE boards and enter premium national colleges like IITs, NITs, and AIIMS. Every student who joins us is given personal attention, continuous assessments, and comprehensive guidance.

"Let us guide your child's curiosity today, so they can confidently lead tomorrow."

Er. Bikash Patel

Managing Director & Founder - Curious Minds Academy

Our Mission

To deliver conceptual, value-based learning modules that inspire curiosity, nurture scientific temperament, and enable students to achieve top ranks in competitive examinations. We aim to make quality tutoring affordable and highly efficient.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a benchmark educational institute in Gujarat, known for coaching excellence, transparent student-centric methodologies, and an outstanding record of toppers. We strive to mold students into responsible, intellectual leaders.

Our Secret Formula

Teaching Methodology

We implement a structured four-pillar approach to ensure conceptual clarity and result-oriented delivery.

01

Concept Lectures

Lectures explaining basic core theories, laws, and equations using real-life examples and animations.

02

Practice Worksheets

Curated Daily Practice Problems (DPPs) and homework banks covering board-level and advanced MCQ criteria.

03

Weekly Evaluation

Strict blueprint-based examinations held every Sunday, giving prompt feedback on students' weaknesses.

04

Personal Remedials

Focused doubt clarification sessions and personalized study plans for students who need extra push.
